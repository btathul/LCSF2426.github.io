Our History

John Devor

Devor Electric Can Openers started in the early 1990s with a mission to revolutionize kitchen convenience. Our company designs, manufactures, and markets high-quality, user-friendly electric can openers that have become a staple in kitchens worldwide. Known for their durability, innovative design, and ease of use, Devor products are trusted by home cooks and professional chefs alike. We pride ourselves on delivering electric can openers that make everyday tasks simpler and safer.

How It All Began

In 1995, John Devor, an engineer and inventor, was frustrated by the complexity of traditional manual can openers. He set out to create a reliable, electric alternative that was not only practical but also affordable for the average consumer. After years of research and development, Devor Electric Can Openers were born. The company quickly gained recognition for its top-tier products and strong commitment to customer satisfaction.

By 1998, the company had expanded its operations to international markets, including Canada, the UK, and Japan. In 2000, Devor introduced the world’s first "One-Touch" can opener, a feature that automatically opened cans with a simple press of a button—setting a new industry standard for ease and reliability. Today, we are proud to serve households and businesses in more than 30 countries.

Innovative Solutions for Everyday Use

Devor's success lies not only in our products but also in our dedication to problem-solving. Our electric can openers are designed with features like automatic shut-off for safety, ergonomic handles for comfort, and easy-to-clean parts. In the early 2010s, we expanded our product line to include battery-operated models, offering even more flexibility for consumers who wanted portability and convenience.

Commitment to Sustainability

At Devor, we understand the importance of sustainability. Our products are built to last, minimizing waste, and we are continually seeking ways to reduce our environmental impact. We use recycled materials in our packaging, and our manufacturing process is designed to conserve energy and reduce emissions.

Staying connected with our customers is key to Devor’s success. We value feedback and actively engage with our community through social media platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter. We also host annual product demonstrations and innovation contests to encourage consumer input and feedback. Devor’s goal is to create products that truly enhance the everyday lives of our customers.

As we continue to grow and innovate, we remain committed to our mission: to create electric can openers that make life easier, safer, and more convenient for everyone.
